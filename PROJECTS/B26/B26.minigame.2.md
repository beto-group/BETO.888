# Find My Love: The Reason Behind It All

In a world shrouded in mist, our hero, Elara, wanders through an endless labyrinth, driven by a yearning for his lost love, Seraphine—or so he believes. Guided by fleeting visions of her radiant smile, Elara's quest is to find her, the one who completes his soul. The game’s title, _Find My Love_, pulses with hope, but every so often, it flickers to _Find My Lust_, hinting at a darker desire twisting within him.

Seraphine appears in dreams as a celestial figure, her voice like starlight, urging him forward. Is she a goddess watching over him, or the mortal love he seeks? The truth is veiled. As Elara navigates the labyrinth, collecting fragments of memories, a plot twist unravels: Seraphine is both his love and a deity testing his heart. Her divine trial forces Elara to confront whether his pursuit is driven by pure love or a selfish craving for possession, blurring the line between devotion and obsession.
